# When the human start thinking

Suppose aap apne life ke kisi timeline per hain, apne us time ek decision liya and ab aap us decision ko 2 saal baad sochate hain, apko samjh aata hai ki aap kitna faltu, unrelevent, unrealistic sochte the, asicaly aap kitne dumb the

## Lekin kya ye sach me sach hai

*Aap apne present me ab tk ke sabse best version me hote hain agar aap khud ko kuch samaya piche rakh kr dekhna chahe to*

Maan lijiye aap saalo pehle kisi conversation me the, apne bahut jada confidence se ek kafi attractive baat boli thi, sabne apka samarthan thoda sa muskura kr kiya
Fir sab apni aage ke conversation me vyast ho gye, lekin kyu apko lg raha tha ki jo apne bola tha kafi had tk sahi tha
Meri maniye to agar aap us baat ko saalo baad aaj soch rhe hain then apko samjh aata hai ki jo us samaya apki baat ko sun ke apke samarthan me muskuya kr apki baat ko accept kiya tha, vo asal me apka majak udaya ja raha tha?

Kyuki apne jab us baat ko aur us mahol ko aaj itne smy baad fir se analyse kiya then apne paya ki us jagah hota to main khud per hi hansa hota hai ki tum jis group me ho us group se kitne immature the.
